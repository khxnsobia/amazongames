About Amazon Games

Amazon Games is growing talented teams in Washington, California, New York, London, Munich, and more.

Seattle is the home of Relentless Studios, the development team behind Crucible, who is working on several exciting projects. The Amazon Games team in Orange County is in production on New World and Lord of the Rings, while our team in San Diego has some promising unannounced projects in development. No matter where you look, we have passionate Amazonians working on ways to deliver exceptional gaming experiences to connect with customers around the world.

Amazon Games' studios

Amazon Games San Diego studio

San Diego, CA

Nestled in the rolling suburban hills of Rancho Bernardo, Amazon Games, San Diego is ideally situated minutes from the vibrant downtown of California’s second largest city. Home to the world’s best beaches, breweries, and burritos.

Relentless Studios

Seattle, WA

Our Seattle teams are located in the heart of downtown and surrounded by great restaurants, parks, shopping, and nearby outdoor recreation. The Seattle area has a vibrant arts community, ridiculously passionate sports fans, and is home to many events including PAX West and The International.

Amazon Games Orange County studio

Orange County, CA

Our studio sits in Irvine, a well-organized city that’s rated as one of the safest places to live in the US. The weather is unbeatable year-round and the education system is world-class. Surf in the morning, ski or mountain bike in the afternoon, or drive a half-hour to Disneyland—Orange County offers a quality of life that’s hard to beat!

What we've been working on

We believe the evolution that began with arcade communities a quarter-at-a-time will develop into a future where everyone is a gamer and every gamer can create, compete, collaborate, and connect with others at a massive scale.

Amazon’s relationship with gamers goes back to 1998 when we began selling video games for the Sega Dreamcast, Nintendo 64, and Neo Geo. Today Amazon Games is creating and collaborating with powerful partners and resources including our in-house game development studios, Amazon Prime, Prime Gaming, Twitch, AWS, and AWS Game Tech.
